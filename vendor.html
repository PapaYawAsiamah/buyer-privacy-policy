Vendor Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 21, 2025

This Vendor Privacy Policy explains how qwikbasket collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal and business information from vendors (restaurants, stores, or merchants) using our vendor platform.

1. Information We Collect

Business & Contact Information

When you register as a vendor, we collect information including:

  • Business Name: The official name of your restaurant or store
  • Contact Person (Full Name): Primary contact for account and operational communication
  • Email Address: For account management and notifications
  • Phone Number: For order coordination and urgent communications
  • Payment Details: Bank or payment account information needed to settle payouts (securely handled by payment processors)

Location Information

We collect location data for purposes such as displaying your storefront to nearby customers, calculating delivery options, and supporting pickup/delivery logistics. Location data may include:

  • Business address and coordinates
  • Optional device-based location if you use our vendor mobile app

Notifications

We may request permission to send push notifications to vendor devices to inform you about new orders, cancellations, and time-sensitive updates. You can manage notification preferences through your device or in-app settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use vendor information to:

  • Create and manage vendor accounts
  • Display your business to customers and accept orders
  • Coordinate order preparation, pickup, and delivery
  • Process payouts and financial reconciliations (through payment processors)
  • Send important operational messages and security alerts
  • Analyze performance and improve marketplace features

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell vendor data. We may share vendor information with:

  • Service Providers: Mapping, payment processors, hosting, and messaging platforms
  • Delivery Partners: Delivery services or riders may receive order-related information necessary to complete deliveries
  • Legal & Compliance: To respond to legal process or protect rights and safety
  • Business Transactions: As part of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with appropriate protections

4. Data Security & Retention

We take reasonable steps to protect vendor data and retain it only as long as necessary for operational, legal, and tax purposes. Payment details used by third-party processors are stored and handled by those processors under their security policies.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Vendors can:

  • Access and correct account information
  • Request deletion of personal information (subject to legal or regulatory retention requirements)
  • Manage notification preferences and device permissions
  • Contact support to close or transfer an account

6. International Transfers

Vendor data may be transferred and processed in countries outside your home country. We use contractual and technical safeguards where required.

7. Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, notified to affected vendors.
